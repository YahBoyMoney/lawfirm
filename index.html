Frequently asked

Honest answers, before you call.

Below are the questions most people ask in the first conversation. There is no obligation, and no charge, to ask the rest by phone.

How much does a consultation cost?

Nothing. The initial consultation is free, and we accept cases on contingency, meaning you pay no attorney fees unless we recover compensation for you.

How long do I have to file a claim in California?

For most personal injury claims in California, the statute of limitations is two years from the date of injury. Claims against public entities can require notice within six months. The clock matters, call sooner rather than later.

What if I've already spoken with the insurance company?

It's common, and not necessarily fatal to a case. We'll review what was said and recorded, take over communications, and stop further contact between you and the adjuster.

Will my case go to trial?

The majority of personal injury claims resolve before trial, but we prepare every file as if it will. That preparation is often what produces a fair settlement.

What does Berhe Law charge?

A standard contingency fee, taken only from a recovery. There is no hourly billing and no charge for the consultation. We discuss specific fee terms in writing before you sign anything.

What if my injury seems minor?

Injuries from soft-tissue damage to concussions often develop over days or weeks. A short conversation is worth having before assumptions are made, for you or by an adjuster.
