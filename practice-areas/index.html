Keep the first message short, factual, and conflict-safe.

Send the basic parties, dates, place, type of harm, and current deadline concerns. Do not upload or paste full medical records, private account numbers, Social Security numbers, or privileged documents through a public form. If a deadline is close, call 909-609-6685 instead of waiting.
