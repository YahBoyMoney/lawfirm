What to save now

Turn the incident into proof before receipts and photos disappear

Strong screening is built on documents. Save originals, screenshots, photos, dates, and expenses. Keep full medical records private until a secure route is provided.

Evacuation costs

Hotel, shelter, gas, rideshare, food, childcare, pet boarding, cleaning supplies, masks, filters, and temporary relocation receipts.

Medical symptoms

Eye or throat irritation, coughing, headache, dizziness, wheezing, rash, urgent care, ER, prescriptions, and vulnerable household impacts.

Wage and business loss

Missed shifts, closed storefronts, cancelled appointments, payroll disruption, inventory loss, refunds, POS reports, and customer messages.

Property and cleanup

Photos of residue, odors, HVAC filters, cleanup supplies, damaged property, testing, remediation, and contractor or cleaner invoices.

Best-fit leads

Who should submit first?

  • Residents with documented symptoms or medical care connected to the evacuation or reported exposure window.
  • Businesses that closed or lost revenue because employees, customers, suppliers, or operations were disrupted.
  • Workers who missed shifts or lost wages because of evacuation, exposure concerns, transportation, or childcare issues.
  • Families with vulnerable members including children, seniors, pregnant residents, asthma/COPD, or immune-compromised household members.
